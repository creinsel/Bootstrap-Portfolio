About Me

Hi! I'm Cassie. I was born in Oakland, California and moved to Hayward, California when I left the hospital. When I was three months old I moved to Iowa with my mom. I stayed there until I was 5, then I came back to Hayward where I lived until I went to college in Los Angeles. After college, I moved to the Big Island in Hawaii and became a math teacher. Besides the relationships I formed with my students, my favorite part of teaching was thinking of creative methods to engage and deliver content to my classes. Look below for an example. When I was on the Big Island I also lived in the Laker House (I'll include a picture of this below as well). When I came back to California, I continued to work in education and public service in different roles. Now I am ready for something new.
